Brand Introduction

YONGSHAO is an integrated brand focusing on design, production and sales. We specialize in household daily goods, home textiles, portable small home appliances and outdoor daily supplies.

We stick to practical, simple and cost-effective design standards, strictly control product quality, and meet daily usage needs for family, office and outdoor scenarios.

Our Strengths
